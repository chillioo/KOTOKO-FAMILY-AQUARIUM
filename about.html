Get to know more about us!

Welcome to Kotoko Family Aquarium, established in 2007, catering exclusively to young members and adults in the family of fish lovers who share passions for vibrant aquatic life. Nestled in the middle of Kuala Lumpur, our store is the realization of a dream by aquatic enthusiasts dedicated to creating a welcoming haven for like-minded individuals. Our store specializes in coral reef fishes and our knowledgeable staffs are here to assist hobbyists on all levels of budgets to suit their preferences. Immerse yourself in our extensive variety of coral reef fishes, reflecting the beauty of the aquatic world at Kotoko Family Aquarium.
